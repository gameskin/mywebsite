The following elements have different font-sizes using px, pt, em, and rem units. You can see how they look on different devices and screen sizes by resizing your browser window or using the developer tools.

This paragraph has a font-size of 14pt.

This paragraph has a font-size of 2em.

This square has a width and height of 10vw.
